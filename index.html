About Cognivex

Built by people who have lived the operational problems we solve

Cognivex was founded by engineers and business professionals who spent years inside manufacturing, procurement, and corporate operations — and grew tired of watching capable teams lose hours to manual, error-prone work.

Our story

From frustration on the shop floor to practical AI in production

We started Cognivex because we kept seeing the same pattern: brilliant people maintaining critical processes in fragile spreadsheets, re-keying the same data, and making high-stakes decisions without the visibility they deserved. The tooling existed — but no one was translating it into something that fit how large organizations actually operate.

So we built a team that bridges both worlds. Our engineers know how to ship secure, scalable software. Our business professionals know how procurement, quality, HR, and operations really run. Together we identify the processes where automation and AI create the most leverage, and we implement them as governed, production-ready systems — not throwaway pilots.

The result is transformation that survives contact with the real world: adopted by teams, trusted by leadership, and defensible to auditors.
